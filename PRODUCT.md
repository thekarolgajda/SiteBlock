# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who struggle with distraction and have proactively blocked sites they know pull them away from what matters. They're not being punished by software — they made this choice themselves, and the blocked page is a moment they created for themselves.

## Product Purpose

SiteBlock helps people protect their attention by blocking sites they've chosen to avoid. The blocked page is the core moment of the product: a pause between impulse and action. Success looks like the user feeling affirmed, not ashamed, and returning to their intended work.

## Brand Personality

Warm, grounding, human. The extension is like a trusted friend who gently reminds you of what you said you wanted. Not a warden. Not a nag. A quiet anchor.

## Anti-references

- Harsh, punishing error-page aesthetics (red warnings, 🚫 icons, alarm language)
- Cold productivity-tool minimalism that feels robotic
- Dark hacker/terminal aesthetics
- Generic "focus app" green-and-white corporate wellness
- Anything that feels like the user did something wrong

## Design Principles

1. **The pause is the product.** The blocked page isn't a dead end — it's an intentional moment. Design it to feel like a breath, not a wall.
2. **Affirm the decision.** The user made a good choice when they blocked this site. The page should echo that back without lecturing.
3. **Warmth over drama.** Nothing alarming, nothing loud. Soft, human, grounded.
4. **Restraint earns trust.** One clear action. No guilt-tripping copy. No extra noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. No motion that can't be paused. Sufficient contrast on soft backgrounds (warm light themes need care). Font sizes comfortable at arm's length.
